About this timer

This timer was developed by Michael Shan from the Sunnynook Toastmasters Club. It is a simple timer for Toastmasters meetings. The timer runs on both mobile devices and PC computers.

The timer is developed with the help of AI. It is open source and available on GitHub. Please feel free to share the timer with your fellow toastmasters or contribute to the project.

If you have any questions or suggestions, please email Michael Shan.
